About Us

INAS SECURITY SERVICES AND CONSULTANCY SDN BHD, established in November 2011, is a licensed provider of security and private investigation services. Managed by experienced professionals, the company is committed to delivering customer-focused security solutions. It integrates advanced technology when needed and operates in alignment with government law enforcement. Its personnel are trained, disciplined, and dedicated to providing reliable and professional services.
