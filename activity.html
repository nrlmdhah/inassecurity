Training & Performance

INAS Security ensures quality service through structured training and continuous performance evaluation. Guards are trained in security protocols, emergency response, and communication. Their performance is monitored regularly to maintain professionalism, reliability, and client satisfaction.

Performance & Activities

The security guard service provided by INAS SECURITY SERVICES AND CONSULTANCY SDN BHD is as follows:

  • Internal Security Guards Services
  • Response Services
  • Alarm Systems & Services
  • Body Guard Services
  • Private Investigation Services
  • Central Monitoring System Services

INAS SECURITY SERVICES AND CONSULTANCY SDN BHD is entrusted by various parties including Government Departments, Statutory Bodies, and Private Sectors for providing security in office buildings, financial institutions, factory areas, godowns, open-air stores, housing, mining sites, and trade expos.

We acknowledge that there are day-to-day challenges, especially concerning the financial and welfare aspects of our workers — particularly the security guards. However, with commitment and collective wisdom, we believe these issues can be addressed over time.

Staff Development

Training & Discipline Enhancement

Training is provided to all workers on their first day of employment and is continued periodically to improve work efficiency and productivity at INAS SECURITY SERVICES AND CONSULTANCY SDN BHD.

Basic training such as marching, spiritual development, and physical training is offered to raise the standard of discipline, work quality, and overall work ethics. The goal is to cultivate disciplined, efficient, and highly motivated employees.

Additionally, lecturers are invited to give talks focused on spiritual growth and personal development for all staff members.
